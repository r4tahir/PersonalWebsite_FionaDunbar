Professional Background
Fiona has been a Lecturer in the Faculty of Mathematics since 2005. Fiona also spreads her passion for math beyond the university community. In 2009, she created a workshop for grade 9 and 10 girls called “Think About Math” in order to encourage them to consider furthering their education in mathematics. The success of this program led to the creation of an annual workshop that brings high school girls together with the University of Waterloo’s math alumni. Fiona is the Chair of the Euclid Math Contest committee and member of the Centre for Education in Mathematics and Computing (CEMC). As a CEMC member, Fiona visits high school students and informs them about math contests and interesting areas of math that they can pursue as a potential career path after high school.
