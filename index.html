About Me

Seasoned Manager and Educator with over 15 years of dynamic leadership experience in a university environment. Highly organized and able to manage multiple projects at one given time. Has a demonstrated ability to communicate effectively with key stakeholders, at all levels, within academic institutions. Has acute technical capabilities and a strong working knowledge of various software platforms, as well as expertise in preparing and presenting demos for sales, training and marketing. A keen and quick problem solver with an aptitude for understanding and supporting customers queries. Brings a solid work ethic to the table which is characterized by a high level of integrity, professionalism, and a passion for helping others. Skilled in Project Management, Guest Lecturing, STEM, Public Speaking, and Panel Moderator. Strong education professional with a Master of Mathematics focused in Combinatorics & Optimization, Specialization in Cryptography from University of Waterloo.
